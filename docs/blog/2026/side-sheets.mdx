## Side Sheet

Ever wanted your sheet to slide in from the side? Now it can! The new [`anchor`](/reference/configuration#anchor) prop lets you dock the sheet to the left or right edge ‚Äî perfect for iPad and tablet layouts.

```tsx
<TrueSheet
  anchor="left"
  maxContentWidth={600}
  detents={['auto', 1]}
>
  <View />
</TrueSheet>
```

On Android and Web, fine-tune the edge margin with [`anchorOffset`](/reference/configuration#anchoroffset).

Learn more in the [Side Sheet guide](/guides/anchor).

## Max Content Width

Take control of your sheet's width on larger screens with [`maxContentWidth`](/reference/configuration#maxcontentwidth). On Android and Web, sheets default to `640dp`. On iOS, the system default is used.

```tsx
<TrueSheet maxContentWidth={500} detents={['auto', 1]}>
  <View />
</TrueSheet>
```

Pair it with `anchor` for the ultimate tablet experience!

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Breaking Change

Heads up! `maxHeight` has been renamed to [`maxContentHeight`](/reference/configuration#maxcontentheight) for consistency with `maxContentWidth`. Quick find-and-replace and you're good:

```diff
- <TrueSheet maxHeight={600}>
+ <TrueSheet maxContentHeight={600}>
```
